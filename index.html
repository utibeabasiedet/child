Why Choose CEC?

We are committed to our core values. We have a child first approach. High quality learning. The best foundation for success in life. Parental support and partnership. Safe, clean, nurturing and enriching environment in your absence

Learn More

Our Mission

We partner with parents in raising children who are physically, mentally, emotionally and socially developed and are fully aware of their gifts and potentials, in order to use them in serving the world. We believe in developing a whole child that is why we have developed a very special evening after school club which has a robust curriculum for children from the age of 5 to 10 years.

Our Vision

is to become a global brand by redefining preschool experiences for growing minds and to become pace setters when it comes to early childhood growth and development.

Our Approach

We use the American Curriculum with a blend of practical life activities from the Montessori curriculum. Welcome to the children’s enrichment center A place where your child feels safe, have fun, learn and grows in your absence.
